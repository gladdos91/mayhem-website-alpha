Enforcement and Strike System

1.1 Strike Levels

Mayhem operates under a progressive strike system.

Offense 1
Warning
Offense 2
Warning
Offense 3
Kick from the server
Offense 4
30-day ban (appealable)
Offense 5
Permanent ban (appealable)
Offense 6
Permanent blacklist from all Mayhem servers (no appeal)

Important: Offense 1-3 will reset after 90 days. If a rule is broken within the 90 days we will proceed to offense 5.

Offense 5 and Offense 6 bans apply across all Mayhem servers case by case.

Offense 1 through Offense 4 warnings are server-specific.

Example of how bans work:

Scenario 1: John was playing Space Engineers steam creative and got banned on SE Steam Creative. This will account for both EOS and Steam Creative but not Survival, Minecraft, Hytale, etc.

Scenario 2: John is playing once again Steam creative and decided that he is going to steal a grid and be a jerk about it. John is now banned from all servers Mayhem owns. Not for stealing the grid, but for being a jerk. If he had just accepted the ban, he would still have access to the servers like survival, minecraft, etc.

1.2 Strike Expiration

Offense 1 through Offense 3 expire after 6 months.

Once an Offense 13 is older than six months, it may not be used toward future punishment.

Offense 5 through Offense 6 never expire and remain permanently on a user's record.

1.3 Staff Authority

Staff members will:

  • Identify the rule that was violated
  • Inform the player of the action taken
  • Log the incident for staff records

Failure to cooperate with staff may result in escalated punishment.

1.4 The Exclusion Rule

The Mayhem staff team reserves the right to immediately remove or ban any player if:

Their behavior consistently harms the server experience

Evidence supports immediate action

The situation requires rapid intervention

This rule supersedes all others.

Logging and Security

2.1 Logged Information

For moderation and security purposes, the following may be logged:

  • User IDs
  • In-game chat, including direct messages
  • Programmable block scripts
  • Grid activity and ownership
  • Block placement and removal
  • IP addresses on Steam Creative servers

2.2 Personal Information

Players may not share:

Passwords

Financial or identification information

Account credentials

Another individual's private or sensitive information

Violations may result in Offense 46, depending on severity.

3

Server Limits

3.1 PCU Limits

PCU limits are strict and non-negotiable.

Steam Creative Servers 300,000 PCU per player
EOS Creative Servers 300,000 PCU per player

Any grid exceeding these limits must be removed.

3.2 Immediate Grid Removal

Staff may remove grids without prior warning if:

  • Simulation speed drops to unacceptable levels
  • A grid is clanging, unstable, or glitching
  • Staff instructions are ignored
  • A grid is repeatedly respawned after being addressed

3.3 Exceptions

Only staff-run and publicly announced events may exceed PCU limits.

Player-run events may not bypass PCU limits under any circumstances.

4

Core Server Rules

4.1 Grid and Blueprint Theft

Blueprint and grid theft is treated as a zero-tolerance offense.

Players may not:

Copy or paste grids without explicit permission

Upload grids they do not own

Retain stolen blueprints

Violations may result in Offense 46, including permanent removal.

4.2 Lag-Causing Creations

If a creation causes server performance issues:

  • The player will be asked to remove it
  • Compliance results in no punishment
  • Refusal results in escalating offenses

A grid does not need to exceed PCU limits to be considered laggy.

4.3 Behavior and Content Standards

Mayhem is intended to be a safe environment for all ages.

The following are prohibited:

Racism, discrimination, or hate speech

Harassment or excessive toxicity

Threats, intimidation, or coercion

Pornographic, obscene, or graphic creations

Severe violations may result in immediate permanent bans.

4.4 Suiting and Hydro-manning

Using a character as a weapon is not allowed.

This includes:

  • Grinding or placing blocks on enemy grids
  • Spawning grids inside turret range
  • Using spectator mode to interfere
  • Blocking exits or trapping players

Violations escalate starting at Offense 1.

4.5 Safe Zone Abuse

Safe zones exist for building purposes only.

The following are prohibited:

  • Combat from or into safe zones
  • Using safe zones as shields or decoys
  • Launching grids or missiles from safe zones
  • Entering safe zones to escape combat

Abuse results in escalating punishment.

4.6 Exploits

Some exploits are allowed. The following are not permitted:

  • God-mode combat grids
  • Void or Nan lights
  • Client or server crash exploits
  • Any exploit that interferes with staff moderation

Lag-inducing weapons or exploits are prohibited.

4.7 Server Crashing

Intentionally degrading or crashing a server is a serious offense.

This includes:

Simulation speed manipulation

Excessive sub grid spawning

Physics abuse

Unauthorized stress testing

Punishment may range from Offense 4 to Offense 6.

4.8 Autonomous Grids

  • Missiles must deploy from a parent grid
  • AI drones must originate from a parent grid
  • Hand-spawning combat drones is considered suiting

Violations escalate from Offense 1.

5

EOS-Specific Rules

5.1 Speed-breaking

The maximum allowed speed is 110 meters per second.

Any grid exceeding 150 meters per second will be automatically removed.

Speed-breaking results in grid removal and escalating punishment.

No Loopholes

Technical wording does not override rule intent

Staff decisions are final

If unsure, ask before acting

If an action feels questionable, do not perform it.

Appeals Process

Appeals are reviewed weekly

One appeal per server

A 30-day cool-down applies after a denial

Some bans are not eligible for appeal.

Appeals may be submitted at: https://forums.mayhem-gaming.com
